Has Your Mom Ever Asked,

"Son, What Should I Cook  Today?"

Come on, We've all been there..

To help you answer this, We created SeeFood.

SeeFood helps you to monitor fruits, vegetables and other eatable items stored in refrigerator. If these items finish it will let you know so that you can buy new one before getting frustated and what you can make of the available food at your home by providing different recipies.

Features

It's not just an image vision app, it's much more than that!

Monitor Your Basket

With our food detection sensors, you can always keep an eye on what's present in your refrigerator basket.

Always available

As our product is IOT enabled, so all of the information is available to you anytime, anywhere.

Recipe Suggestions

Our APIs can always help you to decide about  what to cook and how to cook. So, that you don't need to overthink.

Diet Tracker

Tracks your daily food consumption and helps you to monitor your calories intake so that you can stay in shape.

Custom Notifications

You can setup your own custom notifications. So that the potatoes in your basket never runs out ;)

Multiple Users

Multiple Users in a Family can be use the same SeeFood Cam to monitor their individual health.
